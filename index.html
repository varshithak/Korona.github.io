Corona Virus Symptoms

Coughing And Sneezing

Cover your mouth and nose with a tissue when you cough or sneeze.Throw used tissues in the trash.If you don’t have a tissue, cough or sneeze into your elbow, not your hands

Hot Fever

It’s normal for your body temperature to fluctuate throughout the day. But in general, if you’re an adult and your temperature is above 100.4°F (38°C), you have a fever.

Strong Headacke

Many patients equate severe headache with migraine, but the amount of pain does not determine the diagnosis of migraine.

Shortness Of Breath

Shortness of breath, or dyspnea, is an uncomfortable condition that makes it difficult to fully get air into your lungs. Problems with your heart and lungs can harm your breathing. Some people may experience shortness of breath suddenly for short periods of time.

Confusion

Confusion is a symptom that makes you feel as if you can't think clearly. You might feel disoriented and have a hard time focusing or making decisions.

Sore Throat

A sore throat is a painful, dry, or scratchy feeling in the throat. Pain in the throat is one of the most common symptoms
